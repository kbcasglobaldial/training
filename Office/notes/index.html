Note for Foreign Language IVR   (Disposition – Voicemail)

Foreign Language IVR

We need to exhaust the below mentioned steps:
1. Choose an option (If Any) to convert the IVR from “Foreign Language” to “English Language” which would help to explore further to reach the prospect/operator.
2. If there is no option to change the language then stay on the IVR for 1 minute at the most as it will probably connect to the live person automatically.
3. If Action 2 fails then try and reach the operator by pressing ‘0’ immediately (Do not wait further if IVR repeats itself or connects to a dead air before or after pressing ‘0’)


Note: Agent should not enter notes in any other scenarios apart from the ones mentioned above.

Also, In case of multiple, we only attempt the number dialed by default and dispose as per the outcome.
